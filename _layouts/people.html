Biography

Hi! My name is Seokwon Lee. I received my B.S. and Ph. D. degrees in aerospace engineering from the Department of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering, Seoul National University, Seoul, Republic of Korea, in 2012 and 2019. I am currently working at Chung-Ang University as an assistant professor. Previously, I worked at Cranfield University as a research fellow, and a postdoctoral researcher at the Institute of Advanced Aerospace Technology, Seoul National University, respectively.

My research interests include, not limited to, estimation, optimization, and control theories and their applications to autonomous and intelligent systems.
